About Us

JJ's Food Truck started as a small family idea and grew into one of the community's favorite taco spots. We focus on authentic flavors inspired by Mexican street food and prepare everything with care and tradition.

We take pride in using fresh ingredients, handmade recipes, and serving every guest with a smile. Come taste the difference!
